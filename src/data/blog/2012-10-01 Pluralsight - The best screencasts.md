# 2012-10-01 Pluralsight - The best screencasts

I would like to share the link to the excellent service **Pluralsight** ([http://www.pluralsight.com/](http://www.pluralsight.com/)) - only the best screencasts about different technologies.

I watched a lot of their screencasts. They are very professional and you can get basic (or even deeper) overview of the most actual technologies.

They have a free trial for 200 minutes.

Enjoy!
