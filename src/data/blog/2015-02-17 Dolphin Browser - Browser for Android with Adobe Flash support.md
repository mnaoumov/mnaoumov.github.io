# 2015-02-17 Dolphin Browser - Browser for Android with Adobe Flash support

Hi folks

Sometimes I want to watch movies online from sites that are too lame to switch to HTML5 and still using Adobe Flash plugins. Unfortunately my favorite [Google Chrome for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.android.chrome&hl=en) does not support it well. I tried many browsers that sort-of-support Flash but many of them behave unstably.

Today I found the one that worked fine for me: [Dolphin Browser](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mobi.mgeek.TunnyBrowser&hl=en)

Stay tuned
