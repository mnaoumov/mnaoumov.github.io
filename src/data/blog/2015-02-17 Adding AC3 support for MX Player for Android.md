Hi folks

[MX Player](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mxtech.videoplayer.ad&hl=en) is my favorite video player for Android.

Recently I found that it does not play movies with AC3 audio.

To fix that you can use the custom codec as mentioned here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mxtech.videoplayer.ad&hl=en . I tried and this worked for me.
